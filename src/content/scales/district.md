---

At the district scale, the analysis focuses on the water infrastructure that connects mountain to coast. The historic ditch system, aquifer catchments, and stream corridors define where buffer zones can be established. This is where the four-buffer framework takes spatial form — coastal, riparian, peri-urban, and water collection zones mapped onto the actual landscape of West Maui.
