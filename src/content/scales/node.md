At the node scale, individual buildings demonstrate how the recovery plan works in practice. The 26x26-foot module system, the elementary school design, and the Banyan Tree civic space are each detailed as prototypes. These are not final designs but working models — replicable units that communities can adapt to their own needs and conditions.
