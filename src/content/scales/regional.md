---
title: "Regional"
summary: "Coast-to-peak analysis of West Maui — topography, rainfall, streams, sea level rise, and composite constraint mapping."
---

The regional scale examines West Maui from the coast to the peak of the mountain, analyzing the natural systems that flow mauka (mountain) to makai (sea). At this scale, the framework maps rainfall patterns, stream networks, the historic ditch system, sea level rise projections, and topographic constraints to identify where development is feasible and where natural systems must be preserved.
