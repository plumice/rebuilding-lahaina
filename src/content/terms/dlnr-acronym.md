State of Hawaiʻi Department of Land and Natural Resources -- the state department overseeing public lands, water resources, coastal areas, and conservation in Hawai'i.

DLNR's Division of Forestry and Wildlife provides native riparian species guidelines prioritizing fire-resistant and flood-tolerant species for riparian restoration. The department's Office of Conservation and Coastal Lands contributes sea level rise reporting that informs the coastal buffer framework. DLNR also provides estuarine wetland management guidelines for transitional habitat zones at stream mouths, and coastal species habitat guidelines relevant to the buffer framework's ecological restoration components.
