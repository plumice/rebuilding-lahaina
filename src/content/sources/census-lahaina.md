Census data for the Lahaina Census-Designated Place (2019-2023) is cited throughout the thesis for baseline demographic and housing statistics. Key figures drawn from this source include: over 83% of Lahaina residents were renters before the fire, the fire displaced approximately 6,000-7,000 residents, and housing costs exceeded what service-industry wages could sustain. These statistics inform the thesis's emphasis on renter protections and the 2,200-unit re-densification target.
