The Kahana Well, coming online early 2026 with 0.96 MGD capacity, will reduce but not eliminate the deficit. Even with the Kahana Well operational, the gap between supply and projected demand remains significant, underscoring the need for distributed water infrastructure strategies including rainwater harvesting, ditch rehabilitation, and aquifer recharge to close the shortfall.
