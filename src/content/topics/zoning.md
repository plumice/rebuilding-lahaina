---
title: "Zoning"
summary: "Land use classifications, conflicts, and strategic rezoning to unlock housing recovery."
---

Lahaina's 17 zoning categories create a complex regulatory landscape that both enables and blocks recovery. Some zones (BR, R-2, R-3) naturally support rebuilding, while others (AG, INT, R-1) restrict the density and flexibility needed. This topic covers the zoning analysis, strategic rezoning opportunities, and the policy changes needed to prioritize housing for returning residents.
