---
title: "Recovery"
summary: "Post-disaster planning, global precedents, and the framework for Lahaina's long-term rebuilding."
---

Rebuilding after disaster is about more than replacing what was lost. Global precedents from New Orleans, Kobe, and Chile show that recovery is an opportunity to address the systemic vulnerabilities that made the disaster catastrophic in the first place. This topic covers the recovery framework, phasing strategy, precedent studies, and the equity considerations that must guide Lahaina's path forward.
