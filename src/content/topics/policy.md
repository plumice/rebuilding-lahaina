---
title: "Policy"
summary: "Government regulations, water rights, zoning laws, and the policy landscape shaping recovery."
---

Recovery happens within a regulatory framework. Water rights, zoning codes, historic preservation rules, and federal aid programs all shape what can be built and when. The Stage 2 Water Shortage Declaration, CWRM aquifer designation, and Iao Water Management Rule create specific constraints. This topic covers the policy landscape and identifies where changes are needed to enable equitable recovery.
