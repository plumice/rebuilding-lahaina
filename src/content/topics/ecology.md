---
title: "Ecology"
summary: "Natural systems, native vegetation, wetlands, and the environmental conditions shaping the recovery plan."
---

Lahaina sits within one of the most dramatic environmental gradients on Earth — from one of the world's wettest summits to an arid coastal plain in just a few miles. Understanding these natural systems is essential to recovery. This topic covers rainfall patterns, stream ecosystems, the lost Moku'ula wetland, invasive species that fueled the fire, and proposed ecological restoration.
