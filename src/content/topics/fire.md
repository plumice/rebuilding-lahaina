---
title: "Fire"
summary: "The August 2023 wildfire, its causes, impacts, and the post-disaster recovery framework."
---

The August 2023 Lahaina wildfire was one of the most catastrophic events in Hawaiian history, destroying approximately 2,200 homes and displacing 6,000-7,000 residents. This topic covers the fire event, its root causes in infrastructure and land management failures, and the proposed recovery strategies.
