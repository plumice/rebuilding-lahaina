---
title: "Coastal"
summary: "Sea level rise, managed retreat, and the transformation of Front Street into a public promenade."
---

Lahaina's coastline faces accelerating change. Sea level rise projections show increasing encroachment through 2300, with groundwater inundation threatening foundations before surface flooding arrives. The thesis proposes a coastal buffer strategy based on partial retreat — creating public space and ecological habitat rather than hardening the shoreline. Front Street transforms from a commercial vehicular road into a pedestrian promenade connecting heritage sites.
